Methodology

How the MVP interprets risk

1. Ingest

Each case or bill is stored with state, date, source type, topic, status, and a short explanation.

2. Classify

Documents are labeled as limiting, expanding, or challenging 14th Amendment protections.

3. Weight

Recency, authority, and legislative stage raise or lower each document’s contribution.

4. Explain

Every score is paired with plain-English reasoning and the documents that changed it.
